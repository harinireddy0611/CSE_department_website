FUNDAMENTALS OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

The Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence comprise foundational principles and techniques aimed at creating intelligent systems capable of mimicking human-like behavior. This encompasses various subfields such as machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and robotics. Key concepts include problem-solving, knowledge representation, reasoning, learning, and adaptation. Understanding these basics is essential for developing AI applications across industries, driving innovation, and solving complex problems.

Major Features of Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence

  • Problem-Solving Methods
  • Knowledge Representation.
  • Reasoning and Inference.
  • Machine Learning.
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP).
  • Computer Vision.
  • Robotics.
  • Expert Systems.
