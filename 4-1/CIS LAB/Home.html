CRYPTOGRAPHY AND INFORMATION SECURITY LAB

Cryptography and information security are critical components of modern digital systems, ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of sensitive information. Cryptography involves techniques for secure communication, data protection, and authentication through the use of algorithms and cryptographic keys. Information security encompasses broader measures to safeguard data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction, including security policies, access controls, and risk management practices. Together, cryptography and information security play a vital role in protecting digital assets, maintaining privacy, and mitigating cybersecurity risks in today's interconnected world.

Major Features of Cryptography and Information Security

  • Confidentiality.
  • Integrity.
  • Authentication.
  • Non-repudiation.
  • Access Control.
  • Data Protection.
  • Key Management.
  • Security Policies.
