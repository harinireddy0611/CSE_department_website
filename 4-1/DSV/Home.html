DATA SCIENCE AND VISUALIZATION

Data science and visualization are integral components of modern data-driven decision-making. Data science involves extracting insights and knowledge from complex and large datasets through statistical analysis, machine learning, and other analytical techniques. Visualization complements data science by presenting findings in a visual format, such as charts, graphs, and dashboards, making complex data more accessible and understandable to stakeholders. By combining data science and visualization, organizations can uncover valuable insights, identify patterns, trends, and outliers, and communicate findings effectively to support informed decision-making and drive business growth.

Major Features of Data Science and Visualization

  • Data Collection and Cleaning.
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
  • Statistical Analysis.
  • Machine Learning.
  • Feature Engineering.
  • Data Visualization.
  • Interactive Visualization.
  • Real-time Analytics.
