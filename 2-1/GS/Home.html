GENDER SENSITIZATION

Gender sensitization refers to the process of raising awareness and promoting understanding of gender-related issues, with the aim of fostering equality and respect for all genders. It involves challenging stereotypes, biases, and discrimination based on gender identity and expression, while promoting inclusivity, diversity, and empowerment. Gender sensitization initiatives seek to create a more inclusive and equitable society by addressing issues such as gender-based violence, unequal access to opportunities, and harmful gender norms. By promoting empathy, understanding, and respect for people of all genders, gender sensitization efforts contribute to building a more just and inclusive world for everyone.

Major Features of Gender Sensitization

  • Awareness-raising.
  • Challenging Gender Norms.
  • Promoting Inclusivity and Diversity.
  • Addressing Gender-based Violence.
  • Empowering Marginalized Groups.
  • Education and Training.
  • Policy Advocacy.
  • Collaborative Efforts.
